About Mood Playlist

We're a team of three people who believe that everyone should be able to listen to music comfortably depending on their mood. Unfortunately, not many services, such as Spotify which is the focus of this website, do not offer options for searching specific songs based on the listener's mood. Generally, sites like Spotify focus on playlists that are randomized or based on a specific genre, which doesn't match the emotional experience that a person is having. This makes it harder for any listener to find a song that is most suitable for a particular mood.

That is why we have came up with a web app that allows you to narrow down a list of songs into a specific category of moods. For instance, if you feel sad or depressed, you would want a song that can symphatize with you, or maybe you want a song that expresses happiness. The Mood Playlist app can accomplish exactly that.
